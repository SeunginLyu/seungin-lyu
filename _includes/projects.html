Team AFK: Bomberman Bot

  • TensorFlow
  • Supervised Learning
  • Convolutional Neural Networks(CNN)

Bomberman Bot is a convolutional neural network that plays the classic arcade game, Bomberman. By collecting the team's own play data, the team trained CNN using supervised learning, along with some hardcoded AI sessions. The bot auto-completes first 10 stages of Bomberman game built on Pygame module.
